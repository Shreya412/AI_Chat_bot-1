Computer engineering is the fastest developing discipline in the world. It integrates several fields of electrical
engineering and computer science required to develop computer hardware and software. Computer engineers embed computers
in other machines and systems, build networks to transfer data and develop ways to make computers, faster, smaller and
more capable. The major technical areas of Computer Engineering are : Cybersecurity, Networking, Database Handling,
Artificial Intelligence, Design Automation, Computer software, Embedded Systems.
